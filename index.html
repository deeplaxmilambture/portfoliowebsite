Publications

Optimal Positioning of Small Cells for Coverage and Cost Efficient 5G Network Deployment:

A Smart Simulated Annealing Approach

Publisher: IEEE
Published in: 2020 IEEE 3rd 5G World Forum (5GWF)

With the escalating load on the current 4G network, the requirement for moving up to 5G has shown up. To address the challenges of deploying 5G networks cost-effectively, a Smart Simulated Annealing algorithm is used to optimize the placement of small cell towers, taking into account factors such as buildings and vegetation. The algorithm includes strategies for removing excess cells and strategically relocating others, with two different displacement methods—Random and Probed—being tested. The Probed Displacement Approach demonstrated superior performance in minimizing costs and maximizing coverage with less variation compared to the Random Approach
